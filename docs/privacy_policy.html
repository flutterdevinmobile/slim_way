Privacy Policy for Slim Way

Effective Date: April 27, 2026

Welcome to Slim Way. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

A. Personal Information

  • Account Data: When you sign up via Email or Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Physical Profile: We collect height, weight, age, and gender to calculate accurate health metrics.

B. Health and Fitness Data

  • Activity Data: With your permission, we collect step counts and active energy burned from Google Health Connect and your device sensors.
  • Nutrition Data: We store logs of the food and water you consume within the app.
  • Weight Tracking: We track your current and target weight history.

C. Device Information

  • We collect device model, OS version, and language settings to provide a better user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To calculate your personalized daily calorie and water goals.
  • To provide AI-driven coaching and progress insights.
  • To sync your data across devices using our secure backend.
  • To send notifications for reminders and health milestones.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal or health data to third parties. We only share information in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We use standard infrastructure like Render (for servers) and Google Cloud (for authentication).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law to comply with legal processes.

4. Health Connect Data

Slim Way follows the Google Play Developer Program Policy, including the Health Connect Permissions Policy.

  • We only use Health Connect data to display your activity within Slim Way and provide health insights.
  • We do not use this data for advertising or marketing.
  • Health data is never shared with third parties.

5. Camera Permission

The camera is used only for scanning food barcodes to log nutritional information. No images are stored or transmitted.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. Your data is stored securely on our cloud infrastructure.

7. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Edit: You can view and edit your profile data directly in the app.
  • Account Deletion: You may request full account deletion by contacting us. Once deleted, all your data will be permanently erased.
  • Permissions: You can grant or revoke access to camera and health data at any time via your device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
