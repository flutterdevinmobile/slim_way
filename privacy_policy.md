# Privacy Policy for SlimWay

**Effective Date: April 27, 2026**

Welcome to **SlimWay**. Your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Information
- **Account Data:** When you sign up via Email or Google, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture.
- **Physical Profiles:** We collect height, weight, age, and gender to calculate accurate health metrics.

### B. Health and Fitness Data
- **Activity Data:** With your permission, we collect step counts and active energy burned from **Google Health Connect** and your device sensors.
- **Nutrition Data:** We store logs of the food and water you consume within the app.
- **Weight Tracking:** We track your current and target weight history.

### C. Device Information
- We collect device model, OS version, and language settings to provide a better user experience.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
- To calculate your personalized daily calorie and water goals.
- To provide AI-driven coaching and progress insights.
- To sync your data across devices using our secure backend (Serverpod).
- To send notifications for reminders and health milestones.

## 3. Data Sharing and Disclosure
We **do not sell** your personal or health data to third parties. We only share information in the following cases:
- **Service Providers:** We use standard infrastructure like Render (for servers) and Google Cloud (for authentication).
- **Legal Requirements:** If required by law to comply with legal processes.

## 4. Health Connect Data
SlimWay follows the **Google Play Developer Program Policy**, including the [Health Connect Permissions Policy](https://support.google.com/googleplay/android-developer/answer/13214811). 
- We only use Health Connect data to display your activity within SlimWay and provide health insights. 
- We do not use this data for advertising or marketing.

## 5. Security
We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure. Your data is stored securely on our cloud infrastructure.

## 6. Your Rights and Choices
- **Access and Delete:** You can view, edit, or delete your profile data directly in the app settings. 
- **Account Deletion:** You may request full account deletion by contacting us at support@uz.slimway.com. Once deleted, all your weight and food history will be permanently erased.
- **Permissions:** You can grant or revoke access to camera (for food scanning) and health data at any time via your device settings.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
